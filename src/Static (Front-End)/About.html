ABOUT US

Welcome to LabMate, your ultimate assistant for booking university computer labs quickly, fairly, and efficiently. Designed by students, for students, LabMate ensures that access to limited lab resources is organized based on your faculty, your course, and your needs.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is simple: to give every student fair access to the computing resources they need, without the hassle. Whether you're a design student needing high-end machines, or a CS major prepping for a coding exam, LabMate ensures you never miss a lab session again.

KEY FEATURES

  • You get lab access when you actually need it.
  • Priority is given to those who require specific software or environments.
  • Fair usage across departments.

Computer Booking System

About

The Computer Booking System is a web-based application designed to help students, staff, and library users efficiently reserve and manage computer usage time. It eliminates the need for manual sign-up sheets and ensures fair usage by tracking available computers in real-time.

Users can log in, check computer availability, and book specific time slots that suit their schedules. Administrators can manage bookings, monitor usage, and generate reports on system utilization.

This system improves accessibility, reduces waiting time, and enhances productivity for users by making the computer booking process seamless and transparent.
